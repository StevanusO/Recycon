Welcome to Recycon!

Recycon is one of the websites that selling recycle and secondhand things such ad accessories, houseware, etc., Although it's secondhand, but you don't have to worry because all of the secondhand things we sell is still in good condition. Recycon is created and organized by two BINUS University students since 2022. in Recycon you can buy so many goods with a friendly price to fullfil your needs. Recycon is not just looking for profit by selling the goods, but our website is also participate in save the planet by selling the recycle things to reduce garbage.

In recent years, global warming has become the serious problem in the community, people are trying to find out solutions to stop the global warming, but is "recycle" could stop the global warming? I'm not sure to say yes, but in some research show if recycle can reduce the consumption of energy which means recycle can bring us a climate change. What are you waiting for? Let's start to buy the secondhand and recycle goods from our website and become a person who wants to save the planet, hit the "Show Product" or the search bar, find the product, choose it, buy it, and congratulations! you have save the planet.
